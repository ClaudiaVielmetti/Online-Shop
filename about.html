Our Story & Team

Welcome to Reverse, an all-girl run clothing company that defies traditional standards and empowers women to pursue their passions in skateboarding and action sports. Reverse was founded by a group of determined young women who wanted to change the status quo and create a clothing brand that truly represents and empowers women in action sports. With a shared passion for skateboarding and a desire to break barriers, they set out to create a brand that not only looks great but also promotes self-expression and encourages women to chase their dreams. And so, Reverse was born.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide stylish and high-quality clothing that promotes self-expression and encourages women to break barriers and chase their dreams. From our team to our customers, we strive to empower and inspire a community of strong and confident women..
